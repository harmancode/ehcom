This site has been retired.

For over two decades, this was a place where I shared direct, fluff-free tech how-tos, tips, and tricks, along with notes on other things I found interesting. I kept it completely ad-free and paid for it out of my own pocket, simply hoping it would be useful to others. It was my clean, quiet way of giving back to an internet that gave me so much.

But the web changed. In recent years, this content was heavily scraped by AI bots that use it for profit without even giving attribution. As search habits shifted toward those same bots, independent spaces like this became ghost towns, leeched only by machines.

I have no interest in continuing to feed these bots. There is simply no reason to keep going.

To anyone who ever found real value here over the years, thank you.
