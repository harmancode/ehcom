This site has been retired.

For over two decades, this was a place where I shared direct, fluff-free notes on tech, law, literature, cinema, and philosophy, completely ad-free and out of my own pocket. It was my clean, quiet way of giving back to an internet that gave me so much, hoping to connect with other human beings along the way.

But the web changed. In recent years, this content was heavily scraped by AI bots that use it for profit without ever giving attribution. Because people increasingly interact only with chatbots, independent spaces like this have become ghost towns, leeched only by non-humans.

I have no interest in continuing to feed the bots. There is simply no reason to keep going.

To anyone who ever found real value here over the years, thank you.
