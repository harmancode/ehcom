This site has been retired.

For more than two decades, this was a place where I shared notes on tech and other useful things, hoping they would help someone out. I never ran ads. I kept the information direct, to the point, and entirely free of fluff, bloat, or marketing. The design was always clean. It was my way of giving back to an internet that had given me so much, and maybe it was also a way to connect with other human beings, however remote that possibility was. I paid for the hosting and everything else out of my own pocket just to keep it natural, human, and genuine.

In recent years, conditions changed heavily, entirely out of my control. The content here was heavily scraped by AI bots that used it without ever driving traffic to the site or crediting the source, all while profiting from what they took. People stopped using search engines, which meant they stopped finding places like this, and started engaging only with their AI chatbots. As a result, this place began to feel like a ghost town, leeched only by non-humans.

At this point, there is no reason to keep going. I have no interest in continuing to feed AI bots.

To anyone who ever read something here and found real value in it, thank you.
